Pipeline

How the model works

01
Data ingestion

10 seasons La Liga CSV · 3,800+ matches · shots, SoT, G/Sh, G/SoT per team

02
Feature engineering

5-match rolling averages, venue encoding, opponent label encoding, form stats

03
ML xG prediction

RF + LR ensemble predicts xG for each team, weighted by inverse MAE

04
Poisson scoreline

5×5 scoreline matrix from Poisson(λ=xG), top 5 most likely scores ranked
